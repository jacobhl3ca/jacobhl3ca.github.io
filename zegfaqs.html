My Story

On August 25th 2017, in a quiet room in New Jersey, I was born.

The board members of the Rutgers Art & Design Club were naturally drawn into my allure, and that's when I soared.

We would make up stories, drew me in various styles and situations, and always joked around about me, which enhanced my prescence.

It gave me life, and started a small green flame in my stomach that has only burned stronger ever since.

I spread my message though art, and represent the strongest love, happiness, and kindness that I wish upon all breathing beings.
