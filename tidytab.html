TidyTab

Bulk unpin or close your Safari pinned tabs — in one clean sweep.

Download forMac

Free · macOS · Signed & Apple-notarized · .dmg

A little menu-bar app I built to clear out Safari's pinned tabs when they pile up. It auto-detects every pinned tab and unpins or closes them in one go — no clicking through each one.

One-click cleanupAuto-detects your pinned Safari tabs and clears them all at once.
Lives in your menu barQuiet and out of the way until the moment you need it.
Signed & notarizedDeveloper ID-signed and Apple-notarized, so it downloads and opens cleanly.

Running a sweep? Press Space anytime to stop.
